# ***FoodLens*** 



<i>FoodLens is an AI‑powered food intelligence and sustainability platform designed to address three interconnected problems in today’s food ecosystem:</i>



<i>1. Food freshness uncertainty and waste</i>

<i>2. Unfair pricing and lack of standardization for farmers</i>

<i>3. Environmental impact caused by inefficient food supply chains</i>



<i>Our platform integrates AI analysis, data visualization, health insights (Google Fit), and carbon footprint tracking to create a transparent, fair, and sustainable food system — from farm to fork.</i>







#### <i>**The Problem We Are Solving**</i>



<i>1. Food Waste \& Freshness Uncertainty</i>



<i>\* Consumers and businesses often cannot accurately judge food freshness.</i>

<i>\* Expired or spoiled food leads to massive \*\*economic loss and health risks\*\*.</i>

<i>\* Lack of predictive tools results in \*\*reactive decisions instead of preventive action\*\*.</i>



<i>2. Farmers’ Problem: Unfair Pricing</i>



* <i> Farmers rarely receive a fair price for their produce.</i>



* <i> Pricing is influenced by:</i>
* <i>
&nbsp;   Middlemen</i>

<i>    Lack of quality-based benchmarks</i>

<i>    Absence of standardized freshness grading</i>



* <i>The same crop may sell at drastically different prices despite similar quality.</i>



<i>3. Environmental \& Carbon Impact</i>



* <i> Food waste contributes significantly to:</i>
* <i>
&nbsp;  Carbon emissions</i>

<i>   Water waste</i>

<i>   Excessive transportation and storage costs</i>

* <i>No unified system exists to measure and visualize food-related carbon footprints.</i>







#### <i>**Our Solution: FoodLens Platform**</i>



<i>FoodLens introduces a standardized, data-driven food intelligence system\*\* that benefits \*\*farmers, consumers, businesses, and the environment.</i>



<i>**Core Pillars:**</i>



* <i>Freshness Prediction (AI-based)</i>
* <i>Quality Standardization for Fair Pricing</i>
* <i>Health-Aware Consumption (Google Fit Integration)</i>
* <i>Carbon Footprint Analysis \& Sustainability Metrics</i>





#### 

#### <i>**Key Features**</i>



<i>**AI-Based Food Freshness Analysis**</i>



* <i>Image-based food analysis using computer vision</i>
* <i>Predicts:</i>
* <i>
&nbsp;  Fresh</i>

<i>   At Risk</i>

<i>   Spoiled</i>

* <i>Helps reduce food waste before spoilage occurs</i>



<i>**Graphs \& Data Analytics**</i>



* <i>Interactive visualizations for:</i>
* <i>
&nbsp;  Freshness trends over time</i>

<i>   Food consumption patterns</i>

<i>   Waste reduction metrics</i>

<i>   Carbon footprint comparisons</i>



* <i>Enables data-driven decision making for households and organizations</i>



<i>**Standardization for Farmers**</i>



* <i> Introduces quality-based grading metrics instead of subjective pricing</i>



* <i> Farmers receive pricing aligned with:</i>

1. <i>    Freshness score</i>
2. <i>    Quality indicators</i>
3. <i>    Shelf-life prediction</i>



* <i> Reduces dependency on intermediaries</i>
* <i> Promotes fair, transparent, and consistent pricing</i>



<i>**Google Fit Integration**</i>



* <i> Links food intake data with user health metrics</i>
* <i> Supports:</i>
* <i>
&nbsp;    Nutrition-aware decisions</i>

<i>     Balanced consumption patterns</i>

<i>     Health-conscious food choices</i>

* <i> Encourages a lifestyle where health and sustainability coexist</i>



<i>**Carbon Footprint Tracking**</i>



* <i>Calculates environmental impact based on:</i>
* <i>
&nbsp;Food type</i>

<i> Storage duration</i>

<i> Waste quantity</i>



* <i>Visualizes:</i>
* <i>
&nbsp;Carbon emissions saved</i>

<i> Water and resource conservation</i>



* <i>Aligns with ESG and sustainability goals</i>







#### <i>**Insights \& Analysis**</i>



<i>FoodLens provides powerful insights through analytics:</i>



* <i>Reduction in food waste percentage</i>
* <i>Correlation between freshness prediction and carbon savings</i>
* <i>Farmer income improvement via standardized pricing</i>
* <i>Consumer behavior analysis based on health data</i>



* <i>These insights help:</i>



* <i> Policymakers</i>
* <i> Businesses</i>
* <i> Sustainability researchers</i>
* <i> Agricultural stakeholders</i>







#### <i>**System Architecture (High-Level)**</i>



*<i>Streamlit handles all UI, visuals, and interaction</i>
*<i>FastAPI/Flask acts as the central control layer</i>
*<i>AI models do freshness & spoilage prediction</i>
*<i>OpenAI API adds reasoning, explanation, and insights</i>
*<i>MongoDB stores everything for reuse across pages</i>
*<i>Sustainability engine converts data into carbon & water impact</i>
